Facial Emotion Recognition

Used concept of Transfer Learning for building Facial Emotion Recognition Model.

Face, the most important part of the human body, it helps to distinguish one from another. Surprisingly it has a language of its own similar to hand gestures that defines the behaviour of the person in any situation, facial expression defines the person as a whole. Facial expression plays an important role in many fields from academic to Medical to Investigation. With increase in Human - Computer Interaction and the world is moving towards automation, There is an emphasis on Facial Emotion Recognition System (F.E.R). Thus in this project we reviewed different Convolutional Neural Network (C.N.N) models on the FER2013 (Kaggle Dataset, 2013) dataset and conclude regarding the development of Facial Emotion Recognition System.

Movie Recommender System

Used concept of correlation for building a recommender system.

It is a python based project that helps us to get related movie based on the movie you previously watched. The movie are suggested based on the review giveing by other users. We used Pearson Correlation for calculating the relation between two number. If the movies are related it gives a positive result if not its gives negative or else its give zero as a result. Here is just an prototype with a fixed data set. But this could be usefull with tweeks to other set of data. For the purpose of showing the power of pearson correlation we used a fixed data set.
